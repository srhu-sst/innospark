From Idea to Prototype

InnoSpark 2026 is a high-intensity innovation event organised by the School of Science & Technology, Swami Rama Himalayan University, Dehradun. Innovators collaborate to generate, refine, and present actionable solutions to real-world challenges.

💡

Idea-to-Prototype Mindset

Move beyond classroom learning with practical problem-solving

🤝

Interdisciplinary Teams

Open to students & research scholars from all programs

🌍

Real-World Impact

Focus on technology, healthcare, sustainability & UN SDGs

Theme Areas

Develop projects in any of the following eight specialised domains

1. AI & Emerging Technologies

Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and next-gen tech

2. Healthcare & Biomedical Innovation

Advancing medical technology and patient care solutions

3. Agriculture and Food

Enhancing food security and agricultural efficiency

4. Yoga, Naturopathy, and Ayurveda

Integrating traditional wellness sciences with modern applications

5. Water & Environmental Sustainability

Tackling climate change, water scarcity & ecological preservation

6. Smart Village & Infrastructure

Rural connectivity and sustainable urban planning

7. Social Impact & Rural Development

Community empowerment and social welfare tools

8. Digital Transformation & Industry 5.0

Human-centric evolution of industrial technology

All ideas are encouraged to align with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs)

Download UN Sustainable Development Goals PDF

How to Participate

  1. 01 Register at the Registration Desk (Individual / Team)
  2. 02 Receive confirmation + link to Idea Garage
  3. 03 Submit your innovative idea following the guidelines
  4. 04 If selected, get notification for Idea Presentation round
  5. 05 Submit PowerPoint (7–10 slides)
  6. 06 Deliver your Idea Presentation

GUIDELINES FOR IDEA SUBMISSION

  • Ideas from ANY domain solving any Real World Problem
  • Motivation – Why this idea matters
  • Objective – What you aim to achieve
  • Solution – The problem you're solving
