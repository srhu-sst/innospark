Your Innovation Journey

A structured path from idea to impact — designed to help you refine, validate, and present your solution.

1

Register & Submit Your Idea

Form a team of 1–3 members and submit your idea with a clear problem statement and proposed solution.

2

Screening & Shortlisting

Top ideas are shortlisted based on innovation, feasibility, and real-world impact potential.

3

Final Presentation & Recognition

Present your idea to an expert panel through a structured presentation followed by Q&A. Top teams receive recognition, awards, and further opportunities.

How to Participate

  1. 01 Register at the Registration Desk (Individual / Team)
  2. 02 Receive confirmation + link to Idea Garage
  3. 03 Submit your innovative idea following the guidelines
  4. 04 If selected, get notification for Idea Presentation round
  5. 05 Submit PowerPoint (7–10 slides)
  6. 06 Deliver your Idea Presentation

GUIDELINES FOR IDEA SUBMISSION

  • Ideas solving real-world problems - aligned with upper mentioned themes.
  • Motivation – Why this idea matters
  • Objective – What you aim to achieve
  • Solution – The problem you're solving
